MoonBin 문빈




1998 - 2023


Moonbin 문빈 was a South Korean singer,
actor, dancer and model under the label Fantagio. He was a member of the South Korean Boy group Astro
and its sub-unit Moonbin & Sahna.




Early Life

Moonbin was born on January 26 1998 in Cheongju, Chungbuk Province.
He attended and graduated from Hanlim Multi Art School with focus
in Partical Music.
Moonbin was one of two siblings in his family, the other younger sister,
Moon Sua, who is a member of the South Korean Korean girl group, Billlie.




Caarer

Under the influence of his Mother, Moonbin debuted as a child model and
ulzzang in 2004. His first know appearance was in 2006, where he appeared in TVXQ's music video
for their song "Balloons" portraying a mini U-Know Yunho.
In 2009, he had his first acting role in the korean drama Boys over Flowers
where he played the younger versions of Kim Bum's Character.
Moonbin was a trainee of Fantagio since fifth grade, becoming a full-fledged trainee
in his middle school years.
He participated in the company's boy group project iTeen and was the second trainee
to be introduced with the Fantagio iTeen Photo Test Cut.
Prior to making his debut with Astro, Moonbin and the rest of his group starred in a
web-drama titled To Be Continued.




Death

Moonbin was found dead in his home in the Gangnam of Seoul on the Evening of April 19, 2023.
According to Moonbin's Family, they said Moonbin died of an "heart attack" as he was suffering with health issues.
